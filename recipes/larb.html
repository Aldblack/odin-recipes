Larb Chicken

Larb is a vibrant and flavorful Thai dish, often considered the national dish of Laos. It's a type of salad made with minced meat (such as pork, chicken, or beef) thatâ€™s seasoned with lime juice, fish sauce, fresh herbs (like mint and cilantro), and ground rice. The combination of these ingredients creates a balance of spicy, tangy, and savory flavors. Larb is typically served with lettuce or cabbage leaves for wrapping and can be garnished with additional herbs and vegetables. It's a refreshing and aromatic dish that's popular in Thai and Laotian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) ground chicken
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup lime juice (about 2 limes)
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/4 cup roasted rice powder
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped fresh mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1-2 Thai bird chilies, finely chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 1 small red onion, finely sliced
