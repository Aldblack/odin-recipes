Stir Fry Tofu

Stir-Fried Tofu is a quick and versatile dish made by pan-frying tofu cubes with vegetables and a flavorful sauce. The tofu becomes crispy on the outside while staying tender inside, and the vegetables retain their crunch and vibrant colors. This dish is seasoned with a savory sauce that often includes ingredients like soy sauce, garlic, and ginger, making it a delicious and satisfying option for a healthy meal. Ingredients

  • block firm tofu
  • 2 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 bell pepper sliced
  • 1 cup brocolli
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds
